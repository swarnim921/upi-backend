## 1. What is the UPI Dashboard System?
The UPI Dashboard System is a simple and secure way to manage your money online. It acts like a digital wallet where you can:
*   **Send Money** to friends or shops.
*   **Receive Money** from others.
*   **Check your Balance** anytime.
*   **See your History** of all payments.

---

## 2. How to Register (Sign Up)
If you are new, you need to create an account.

1.  Click on the **"Register"** button.
2.  Enter your **Name** (e.g., John Doe).
3.  Enter your **Email Address** (e.g., john@example.com).
4.  Create a secret **Password**.
5.  Click **"Submit"**.
6.  The system will give you a unique **UPI ID** (like `john@upi`). This is your digital address for money.

---

## 3. How to Login
Once you have an account, you can log in.

### Option A: Email & Password
1.  Enter your **Email**.
2.  Enter your **Password**.
3.  Click **"Login"**.

### Option B: Login with Google
1.  Click the **"Sign in with Google"** button.
2.  Select your Google account.
3.  You will be logged in automatically!

4.  You will see your Dashboard with your current balance.

---

## 4. How to Send Payments
Sending money is easy!

1.  Click on the **"Send Money"** button.
2.  Ask the other person for their **UPI ID** (e.g., `shop@upi`).
3.  Type their UPI ID in the box.
4.  Type the **Amount** you want to send (e.g., 500).
5.  Click **"Pay"**.
6.  **Security Step:** You will get a code (OTP) on your phone. Enter this code to confirm.
7.  **Done!** You will see a "Success" message, and the money will be sent instantly.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting (Help)

### "User not found"
*   **Meaning:** The UPI ID or Email you typed does not exist.
*   **Fix:** Check the spelling carefully.

### "Insufficient Balance"
*   **Meaning:** You don't have enough money in your wallet.
*   **Fix:** Add money to your wallet or send a smaller amount.

### "Invalid OTP"
*   **Meaning:** The security code you entered is wrong.
*   **Fix:** Request a new code and try again.

### "Payment Failed"
*   **Meaning:** Something went wrong with the internet or system.
*   **Fix:** Wait a few minutes and try again. If money was cut, use the **Refund** button in your History.
